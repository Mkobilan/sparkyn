#### Explain how each product and scope works (Max 1000 chars)
**Sparkyn** is an AI automation platform designed for business growth. Our integration with TikTok uses the following kits:

1. **Login Kit**: We use this to securely authenticate users and verify their identity. We request the 'user.info.basic' scope to personalize the user's dashboard and display their TikTok profile inside Sparkyn.

2. **Content Posting API (Video Kit)**: This is the core of our platform. Users provide a business profile, and our AI generates tailored video content. We use the 'video.upload' scope specifically to allow our automated scheduler to publish these AI-generated reels and videos to the user's TikTok account at their chosen times. This enables businesses to maintain a consistent social presence without manual intervention.

All data is handled according to our Privacy Policy, ensuring tokens are encrypted and no unauthorized activity occurs on the user's account.
